Stack Benefits

Health & Wellness

We care about you and your family’s health, so we offer the best health, dental, and vision benefits to our US and UK employees. We also offer paid maternity and paternity leave. If you enjoy being active, we offer reimbursement for gym membership and other fitness activities. If you work in one of our high-tech offices, you’ll be treated to a healthy, gourmet lunch every day.

Compensation

We believe every employee should share in our success, so we offer competitive salaries, stock options, and retirement plans with a company match. For many roles, we publish a salary calculator.

Education

We actively encourage continued education. From teams sharing knowledge internally to external training and formal tuition reimbursement, we value a team that’s striving to always be better.

Workspace

We do everything possible to help our teams succeed and feel valued, so we make sure you have the tools and workspaces you need. Ergonomic Aeron chairs and Steelcase desks are standard issue.

Enjoyment

We believe teams perform their best when people enjoy their jobs and the people they work with. We offer multiple ways for team members to interact with each other after-hours, whether you work in the office or remotely.

Work Where You Want

We have offices in New York, London, and Denver.
For many teams and openings, we also offer remote work.
